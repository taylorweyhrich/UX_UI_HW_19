about me

Hi, my name is Taylor. I am a highly skilled photographer with over 10 years of experience. I have always been drawn to almost anything creative. During my time at the University of Oregon, I became educated in Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustator, Adobe Premiere, and Adobe Lightroom. Since graduating college, I have designed some illustrations by hand in Procreate. Through my many creative process, I have become a very detail-oriented person. I learn fast and am very adaptable to change. Through my many years in consumer experience, I have great communication skills, enjoy helping others and working with others. Most recently, I have completed a UX/UI bootcamp through the University of Oregon. Utilizing many application like Figma, Miro and GitHub. Collaborating with classmates to make beautiful user experiences. I hope you enjoy looking at my portfolio!
